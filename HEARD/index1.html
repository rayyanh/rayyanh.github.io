What we do and how it works

HEARD is the global leader in sexual assault reporting for women. Simply put, we are for you when you are ready to tell your story. Go ahead and write what you want and share it with who you want. Don’t worry if you want to change your sharing preferences you will always be able to release the information you want at a later date. We know that reporting violence isn’t easy, but we hope to make it as comfortable of an experience as possible. We also promise that your submission will never be censored or changed by any government agency, police force or otherwise given out. Your story is yours and you should be able to tell it. By you doing this, the world will know how prevalent sexual assault really is and only then can we address the full breadth of the problem, once it's understood. So we’re here to listen and to help. You will be heard.
